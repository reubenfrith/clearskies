✅ The Solution

ClearSkies automates the entire workflow

1
Detect — Monitors weather every 5 min against OSHA thresholds at every job site
2
Identify — Uses Geotab geofence zones to find exactly which vehicles are on-site right now
3
Alert — Sends targeted in-cab messages via the Geotab TextMessage API — only to on-site drivers
4
Log — Creates an OSHA-compliant hold record with weather snapshot, vehicles & GPS positions
5
Clear — Issues all-clear automatically when the hold expires, or manually from the dashboard
